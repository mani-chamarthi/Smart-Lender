About Smart Lender

Smart Lender is an AI-powered loan approval prediction system designed to help financial institutions and applicants make faster and smarter lending decisions.

Our Mission

To simplify loan eligibility assessment using Machine Learning while improving transparency, speed, and accessibility.

How Smart Lender Works

1

Applicant information is entered into the system.

2

3

Prediction

The system predicts approval status and confidence score.
